How It Works

A qualitative pipeline, step by step

Every step is human-in-the-loop. AI accelerates the process, but our researchers oversee, validate, and interpret at every stage.

01

Interview scheduling

AI-powered

Our AI scheduling assistant reaches out to your list of participants — stakeholders, service users, staff — and arranges interviews at times that work for them. No back-and-forth emails, no admin burden on your team. You provide the contacts, we handle the rest.

02

Compliance and consent

AI-powered

Before any interview takes place, our platform manages the paperwork. Participants receive consent forms, data handling agreements, and privacy notices automatically. Everything is logged and stored in line with GDPR, giving you a clear audit trail from day one.

03

Literature review

AI + Human

AI accelerates the search and synthesis of existing evidence — scanning databases, surfacing relevant studies, and identifying gaps. Our researchers guide the scope, assess quality, and ensure the review is grounded in methodological best practice.

04

Surveys

AI-powered

We design, deploy, and manage surveys tailored to your evaluation questions. Responses are collected and analysed in real time, with findings fed back to you as they emerge — so you're not waiting until the end to see what participants think.

05

Conduct interviews

Human-led

This is where we stay deliberately human. Our evaluation specialists lead every interview themselves — building rapport, following up on nuance, and adapting in real time. We don't believe the most important conversations in an evaluation should be handed to a chatbot.

06

Additional qualitative data

AI-powered

If you have other documents or qualitative data that needs including — case files, monitoring reports, programme documentation — we feed it into the same analytical pipeline. This lets us triangulate findings across multiple sources, giving you a richer and more robust evidence base.

07

Transcript analysis

AI + Human

Interview recordings are transcribed and processed using AI on secure, GDPR-compliant infrastructure. The system runs qualitative coding — identifying themes, patterns, and sentiment across your full dataset. Our researchers review and validate every output, and early insights are shared with you as they emerge to support decision-making in real time.

08

Evaluation report

AI + Human

This is where the human touch matters most. We synthesise everything into a clear, rigorous evaluation report designed for decision-makers. Findings are grounded in evidence, structured around your evaluation questions, and shaped by the interpretive judgement that only experienced evaluators can bring. Delivered in time to actually inform the decisions it was commissioned to support.
